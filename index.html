Careers paths I've taken

I've pinballed between a couple different careers in 2 short years. I've gone from carpentry, to celltower technician, and currently trying to get into software development. I'm lucky to have been able to figure out what career I wanted while I'm young. At the same time I've been able to gain valuable experience in two vary different fields that most people wouldn't have.

Carpentry

I started an interest in carpentry after my basement had flooded and we re-did the floors. I had stopped doing any schoolwork entirely seeing it as unnecessary to the field, which was a bad decision. At 16 I had joined a vocational school for their building construction program. I was in the program for two years and received a heavy machinery certification and an OSHA 10 safety certification. After graduating I worked as a general laborer for 2 months at a historic renovation company in Saint Louis before being let go.
